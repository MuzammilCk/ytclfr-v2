## Design Context

### Users
The primary users are everyday YouTube viewers who want to extract information from videos. The context of use is broad, so the experience needs to be highly accessible and require zero learning curve.

### Brand Personality
Neutral and unopinionated out of the box, with a calm, organic, and highly functional feel.

### Aesthetic Direction
Currently established as an "ambient light theme" featuring earthy green tones (e.g., `#15724c`), very soft shadows, frosted glass effects (`backdrop-filter: blur`), and generous border radii (`22px`). The aesthetic will expand to support a cohesive dark theme in the future while maintaining the soft, rounded aesthetic.

### Design Principles
1. **Familiarity First:** The application serves a broad audience. Keep layouts predictable and use standard interactions to ensure it feels intuitive to typical web users. 
2. **Ambient & Calm:** Lean into the soft shadows, subtle background gradients, and earthy tones to create a relaxing experience amidst data extraction.
3. **Adaptive UI:** Build components and color tokens with dual theming in mind, ensuring the transition to the future dark mode feels seamless and retains the software's organic softness.
